    What Data do I collect? #

    Log-Files #

    DRKSLV-Discussion follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. All hosting companies do this and a part of hosting services’ analytics. The information collected by log files include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

    The purpose of that information is for analysing trends, administering the site, tracking the audience’s movement on the website, and gathering demographic information.

    User-Account #

    When you register a User-Account with me, I store:

    • A pseudonymous username
    • Your email address
    • whether you have verified your email address
    • whether you have been granted Moderator-Rights
    • your password in encrypted form (bcrypt hashing algorithm)
    • date and time of creation
    • date and time of last edit

    Only your Username, Moderator-Rights and the Date of Creation will be shown publicly.

    This Information is used to provide a public identity to the people using my site. Your email address and Password are used to prove your identity.

    I reserve my right to terminate your User-Account at any Time, for any Reason.

    Posts, Comments and Votes #

    You can voluntarily choose to participate in discussion on my platform. This means creating Posts, Comments and Voting on other users’ contributions.

    Your Posts and Comments store their Creation and Edit timestamps, as well as their author (which associates them with your account) Posts have a title and a text body, Comments only have the text. Comments store their position in the comment hierarchy and which post they belong to.

    You can edit and correct the text body of posts and comments.

    When you delete a post or comment, it is de-associated with your account and the text-body will be deleted. However, post titles and comment hierarchies will be persisted to provide context to other users contributions that were replying to your post or comment.

    Because titles are persisted, please don’t use them to convey sensitive personal data!

    Votes store the date and time you voted, the content you were voting on, and are associated with your account. You can withdraw a vote at any time, and when you delete your account all your votes will be lost as well.
